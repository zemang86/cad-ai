# DBKL (Dewan Bandaraya Kuala Lumpur) Requirements

## Plot Ratio

- Maximum plot ratio capped at **1:10** under KLCP 2020
- CBD sites: up to **1:8**
- Suburban residential: as low as **1:3**
- Exceeding allowable plot ratio: **development charge** required (PPL 1039)

> **Citation**: Kuala Lumpur City Plan 2020 (KLCP 2020)

## Height Control Zones

- DBKL City Planning System (CPS) provides geospatial data layers for:
  - Land Use Zone
  - Intensity (density and plot ratio)
  - Height Control Zone
  - Riverside Corridor
  - Heritage Zone
  - Transit Planning Zone

> **Citation**: DBKL CPS Portal (cps.dbkl.gov.my)

## Parking Standards (DBKL)

| Parameter | Minimum |
|-----------|---------|
| Parking bay width | **2.4 metres** |
| Parking bay length | **4.8 metres** |
| Aisle width | **3.7 - 7.3 metres** (varies by angle) |
| Ramp gradient (steep) | **1:10** |
| Ramp approach gradient | **1:25** |
| Typical residential | 2 lots/unit + **10%** visitor |
| TOD projects | reduced to **1:1** (1 lot per unit) |

> **Citation**: DBKL Guidelines for Car Parking and Internal Traffic Circulation 2014

## Submission Requirements

- All submissions through **One Stop Centre (OSC)** at Menara DBKL II
- Governing legislation: Act 133, Act 171, Federal Territory Building By-Laws 1985, Act 267
- Non-compliance penalties: compound fine up to **RM50,000** and daily fine of **RM500** (Section 26, Act 267)

## Qualified Persons

| Development | Required Professional |
|-------------|---------------------|
| Developments >= **5 acres** | Certified Town Planner |
| Petrol stations, telecom, billboards | Certified Engineer |
| Residential (max 2 storeys, <=300 sqm) | Draftsman permitted |
| Landscape plans | Registered Landscape Architect |

> **Citation**: DBKL submission guidelines
