# Selangor PBT Requirements

## Common Selangor Framework

- Governed by **Selangor Uniform Building By-Laws 1986 (UUKBS)**
- All construction requires written permission under Section 70(1) of Act 133
- Construction can only begin after plan approval and submission of **Form B**

> **Citation**: Selangor Uniform Building By-Laws 1986 (UUKBS)

## MBPJ (Majlis Bandaraya Petaling Jaya)

- Local plan: **Rancangan Tempatan Petaling Jaya 1 & 2**
- Standard renovation plan types (Setara Plans):
  - **Setara Plan 1**: Kitchen extension for terrace houses
  - **Setara Plan 2**: Kitchen extension + toilet
  - **Setara Plan 3**: Front roof extension
  - **Setara Plan 4**: Shared carport roof extension

> **Citation**: MBPJ Building Control guidelines

## MBSA (Majlis Bandaraya Shah Alam)

- Building plan approval charter period: **30 days**
- Primary planning framework: **Shah Alam Local Plan 2035**
- Required documents: cover letter from architect/engineer/draftsman, processing fee per UUKBS, completed Form A, 5 copies of building plan (4 paper, 1 linen), 2 copies of proposed plan in colour (A0/A1)
- Industrial/commercial: additional approvals from **BOMBA**, **DOSH**, and **DOE** required

> **Citation**: MBSA Building Plan Application guidelines

## MPKJ (Majlis Perbandaran Kajang)

- OSC timeline: **3 months** for high-impact/foreign/government projects, **6 months** for others
- Key legislation: Road, Drainage & Building Act 1974, Local Government Act 1976, Strata Title Act 1985
- Required documents: cover letter, processing fee per UUKBS, Form A, JBG forms, title copy, assessment rate receipt, quit-rent receipt

> **Citation**: MPKJ OSC Unit guidelines
