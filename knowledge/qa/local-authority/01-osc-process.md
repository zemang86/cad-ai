# One Stop Centre (OSC) Process

## Overview

The OSC was launched on **13 April 2007** by KPKT for all local authorities. **OSC 3.0 Plus Online** started **1 July 2019**, extended to all **89 local authorities** in Peninsular Malaysia.

## Timeline Targets

| Project Type | Target Duration |
|-------------|----------------|
| Build-Then-Sell (BTS) / High-impact / Foreign investment / Government | **4 months** |
| Sell-Then-Build (STB) | **6 months** |

> **Citation**: KPKT OSC 3.0 Plus Guidelines

## Process Flow

1. Online submission via OSC portal
2. Review and comments by technical agencies
3. Fee payment
4. Hardcopy submission
5. Final approval

- Real-time tracking via **C4i** system
- OSC processes land development applications, planning permission, and building plans **simultaneously**

> **Citation**: KPKT OSC 3.0 Plus Guidelines

## Technical Agencies Involved

**Internal** (within PBT):
- Planning Department
- Building Department
- Engineering Department
- Landscape Department

**External**:
- Land & Mines Office
- Water Authority
- **IWK** (sewerage)
- **JPS** (drainage)
- **BOMBA** (fire safety)
- **DOE** (environment)
- **TNB** (electricity)
- **JKR** (public works)

> **Citation**: KPKT OSC 3.0 Plus Guidelines
