# Part IX — Miscellaneous

## By-law 254: Buildings to Which Parts VII and VIII Apply

Existing buildings which on the date of commencement of the By-laws have been erected (or are being erected, or have approved plans) and fall within: Place of Assembly, Shop, Office, Other Residential, buildings exceeding **18.5 metres**, or hazardous/special risks — must be modified to comply with Parts VII and VIII within:

- **(a)** **1 year** — buildings up to 3 storeys
- **(b)** **3 years** — buildings exceeding 3 storeys

> **Citation:** By-law 254, UBBL 1984

## By-law 255: Power of Local Authority to Extend Period

- Local authority may grant extension(s) of the compliance period under By-law 254
- Local authority may allow variations, deviations or exemptions from Parts VII and VIII
- Any aggrieved person may appeal in writing to the Minister/State Authority within **30 days** of receipt of the decision; the Minister's/State Authority's decision is final

> **Citation:** By-law 255, UBBL 1984

## By-law 256: Buildings Exempted

Parts VII and VIII (except By-laws 134, 141, 225(2), and 227) **do not apply** to:

- Private dwelling houses (detached, semi-detached, terrace) intended for **single family occupancies**

> **Citation:** By-law 256, UBBL 1984 (Amendment 2012/2021)

## By-law 257: Application of Standard or Code of Practice

- Where any standard or code of practice is referred to in the By-laws, and a corresponding **Malaysian Standard (MS)** or other acceptable standard is subsequently published, the Malaysian Standard shall be deemed to have **superseded** the referenced standard
- Compliance with the superseding standard is required

> **Citation:** By-law 257, UBBL 1984 (Amendment 2012/2021)

## By-law 258: Failure to Buildings

In the event of any failure to a building (during erection or after completion), the principal submitting person or submitting person who:
- submitted the plans/drawings/calculations
- supervised the setting out
- certified the setting out
- supervised the erection
- certified proper supervision

Shall within **1 week** of the failure (or further period specified by the local authority):
- **(i)** Report the failure
- **(ii)** Explain the cause
- **(iii)** If during erection, state remedial action taken

Further information may be required as specified by the local authority.

> **Citation:** By-law 258, UBBL 1984 (Amendment 2012)
