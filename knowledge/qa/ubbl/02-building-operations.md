# Building Operations and Permits

## By-Law 16: Commencement of Building Operations

- No building operations shall be commenced without written approval from the local authority (UBBL By-Law 16(1))
- Approval lapses if building operations do not commence within **12 months** from the date of approval (UBBL By-Law 16(2))
- An extension may be applied for before the expiry period (UBBL By-Law 16(3))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part II, By-Law 16

## By-Law 25: Certificate of Completion and Compliance (CCC)

- Upon completion, the principal submitting person (PSP) shall issue a CCC (UBBL By-Law 25(1))
- The CCC certifies that the building has been constructed in accordance with the approved plans (UBBL By-Law 25(2))
- The CCC must be accompanied by:
  - Form F (certificate by architect/engineer)
  - Form G1-G21 (certificates by specialist contractors)
  - Fire certificate (for designated buildings)

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part II, By-Law 25

## By-Law 27: Supervision of Works

- Building works must be supervised by the PSP or a competent person under the PSP (UBBL By-Law 27(1))
- Structural works must be supervised by a Professional Engineer (UBBL By-Law 27(2))
- The PSP shall keep proper records and site diaries (UBBL By-Law 27(3))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part II, By-Law 27

## By-Law 29: Temporary Structures

- Temporary structures require approval from the local authority (UBBL By-Law 29(1))
- A temporary structure shall not remain erected for more than **12 months** (UBBL By-Law 29(2))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part II, By-Law 29
