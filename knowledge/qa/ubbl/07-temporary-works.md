# Temporary Works in Connection with Building Operations

## By-Law 48: Hoarding

- Every building site shall be enclosed by a hoarding or fence during construction
- Hoarding must be of minimum **2.0 metres** height
- Hoarding along public roads must be maintained in good condition throughout construction
- Adequate lighting required where hoarding obstructs public pathways

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IV, By-Law 48

## By-Law 49: Scaffolding

- Scaffolding must be erected and maintained in accordance with safe working practices
- No scaffolding shall be erected on a public road without written permission from the local authority
- Scaffolding must be inspected by a competent person at regular intervals
- Platform widths must be adequate for the work being carried out

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IV, By-Law 49

## By-Law 50: Overhead Protection

- Where building operations are carried out adjacent to public roads or footways, overhead protection shall be provided
- Protection must be of adequate strength to withstand falling materials

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IV, By-Law 50

## By-Laws 51-52: Site Safety

- Adequate precautions must be taken to prevent danger to persons on or near the building site
- Materials must not be deposited on public roads without permission
- Any damage to public infrastructure must be repaired at the builder's expense
- Local authority may order removal of any temporary structure that poses a hazard

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IV, By-Laws 51-52
