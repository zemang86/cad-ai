# General Provisions

## By-Law 2: Definitions

Key definitions under UBBL:
- **Building**: Any structure whether permanent or temporary erected on any land (UBBL By-Law 2)
- **Building operations**: Erecting, re-erecting, or making alterations to a building (UBBL By-Law 2)
- **Designated building**: Buildings which require a fire certificate under the Fire Services Act 1988 (UBBL By-Law 2)
- **Fire wall**: Any wall (not external) having fire resistance as required under Part VII, used for separation of adjoining buildings to prevent/reduce fire spread
- **Qualified person**: A Professional Architect or Professional Engineer registered under their respective Acts

### 2021 Amendment — New and Amended Definitions

- **Principal submitting person**: The Professional Architect or Professional Engineer who submits plans and takes professional responsibility (amended — expanded role under CCC)
- **Contractor**: Person or company carrying out building operations (new definition)
- **Construction project manager**: Person appointed to manage and coordinate construction works (new definition)
- **SPAH (Sistem Penuaian Air Hujan)**: Rainwater harvesting system — new defined term
- **Disabled person**: Person with long-term physical, mental, intellectual, or sensory impairment (new definition per Persons with Disabilities Act 2008)
- **Smoke door**: *Deleted* from definitions in 2021 Amendment (replaced by specific fire shutter/fire door provisions)

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part I, By-Law 2; Amendment 2021

## By-Law 3: Classification of Buildings

Buildings are classified by purpose group:

| Group | Description | Examples |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| I | Small residential | Detached, semi-detached, terrace houses (up to 4 storeys) |
| II | Institutional | Hospitals, care homes, homes for elderly |
| III | Other residential | Hotels, hostels, boarding houses, flats |
| IV | Office | Office buildings, banks |
| V | Shop/commercial | Shops, malls, markets, restaurants |
| VI | Factory/warehouse | Factories, workshops, warehouses |
| VII | Place of assembly | Cinemas, theatres, places of worship |
| VIII | Storage/special | Car parks, petrol stations |

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part II, By-Law 3

## Part II: Submission of Plans (By-Laws 3-29)

### Key Submission Requirements

| By-Law | Subject | Requirement |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| 3 | Submission of plans | All plans submitted to local authority with prescribed fees and Form A |
| 5 | Supervision of work | Must be supervised by Principal Submitting Person (PSP); **2021: construction project manager and site supervisor also required** |
| 6 | Plans to be signed | Signed by PSP AND property owner (or agent) |
| 8 | Plans copies | Plans must be deposited — **2021: "as the local authority may determine"** (previously required triplicate) |
| 9 | Scale of plans | Prescribed scales for drawings |
| 10 | Plans required | Must show dimensions of building, rooms, wall thickness, doors, windows, daylight areas, air-wells, open spaces, foundation walls, piers, stanchions, names and use of rooms. **2021: must also show SPAH layout for buildings with roof area ≥100 sq m** |
| 14 | Plans of alteration | Removed parts in dotted lines; new work highlighted in **red** |
| 16 | Approved plans | No person shall erect any building except in accordance with approved plans |

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part II, By-Laws 3-29; Amendment 2021

### By-Law 10: SPAH (Rainwater Harvesting) — 2021 Amendment

- All buildings with **roof area ≥100 sq metres** must install a **SPAH** (Sistem Penuaian Air Hujan / Rainwater Harvesting System)
- SPAH details must be shown on submitted plans
- System must comply with requirements under By-Law 115 (constructional requirements for SPAH)

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 10

### By-Law 25: Certificate of Completion and Compliance (CCC) — 2021 Overhaul

The **Certificate of Completion and Compliance (CCC)** replaced the CFO via the 2007 Amendment to Act 133. The 2021 Amendment significantly expanded By-Law 25 with **20 paragraphs** covering:

**CCC Process (2021):**
- The **Principal Submitting Person** (architect/engineer) self-certifies compliance
- The local authority no longer issues the occupancy certificate
- The PSP takes full professional responsibility for compliance

**Forms G1–G21 (2021 Amendment):**
- **Form G1**: PSP certification of completion and compliance
- **Form G2**: Structural engineer's certification
- **Form G3**: M&E engineer's certification
- **Form G4–G5**: Earthworks and geotechnical certification
- **Form G6–G10**: Fire safety certifications
- **Form G11–G15**: Infrastructure certifications (TNB, IWK, water, telecom, drainage)
- **Form G16–G21**: Specialist certifications (lifts, SPAH, accessibility, environmental)

**Multi-Agency Sign-Off Required:**
- **JBPM (Bomba)**: Fire safety compliance confirmation
- **DOSH**: Occupational safety for applicable building types
- **TNB**: Electrical supply connection and compliance
- **IWK**: Sewerage connection and compliance
- **JKR**: Road and drainage infrastructure (where applicable)

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, By-Law 25; Amendment 2021; Street, Drainage and Building Act 1974 (Amendment 2007), Section 70
