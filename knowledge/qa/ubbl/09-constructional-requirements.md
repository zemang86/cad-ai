# Constructional Requirements

## General (By-Laws 81-100)

- All materials used in construction must comply with relevant Malaysian Standards
- Where a Malaysian Standard (MS) exists equivalent to a British Standard (BS), the MS shall prevail (By-Law 257)
- Concrete works must comply with MS 1195 or equivalent
- Steel structures must comply with MS 1553 or equivalent

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VI, By-Laws 81-100

## By-Law 86: Party Walls — 2021 Amendment (REPLACED)

The 2021 Amendment replaced By-Law 86 with enhanced acoustic and fire requirements:

- Party walls between adjoining buildings must achieve **STC 50** (Sound Transmission Class 50) acoustic rating
- Minimum construction:
  - **≥200mm** stone/brick/block wall, OR
  - **≥100mm** reinforced concrete wall
- Party walls must also achieve the fire resistance period required by Part VII for the building's purpose group
- Damp-proof courses required at ground level

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 86

## Wall Construction

- External walls must be of adequate thickness to resist weather penetration
- Minimum wall thickness varies by material and building height
- Damp-proof courses required at ground level

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VI

## By-Law 99: Cooking Facilities — 2021 Amendment

- Kitchen exhaust ducts in non-residential buildings must be constructed of **refractory material** and extend to the **roof level**
- Exhaust duct must have minimum **2-hour fire rating**
- Duct must not pass through any fire compartment wall without maintaining the wall's fire integrity
- Grease filters required at point of extraction

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 99

## By-Law 106/107: Room Dimensions — 2021 Amendment

- By-Law 106: Minimum internal dimensions for habitable rooms updated to align with By-Law 42 values
- By-Law 107: Minimum dimensions for non-habitable rooms (stores, utility rooms) — minimum **1.5 sq metres** floor area

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Laws 106, 107

## By-Law 109: Spiral Staircases — 2021 Amendment

- Spiral staircases are **banned from use as means of escape**
- May only be used as secondary/convenience staircases within a single occupancy unit
- Not permitted in any fire escape route or as the sole means of access to any floor

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 109

## By-Law 115: Roof Coverings, Drainage, and SPAH

- Roof coverings must be of incombustible or fire-resistant materials for buildings requiring fire resistance
- Adequate roof drainage must be provided to prevent water accumulation

### SPAH (Rainwater Harvesting) — 2021 Amendment (Expanded)

- **SPAH (Sistem Penuaian Air Hujan)**: Required for all buildings with roof area **≥100 sq metres** (see By-Law 10)
- SPAH piping must be **painted green** for identification — distinct from potable water (blue) and fire (red)
- Pipes and tanks must be marked **"No For Drinks"** or **"Air Bukan Untuk Diminum"**
- **Cross-contamination prevention**: SPAH system must have an air gap or backflow prevention device between harvested water and potable water supply
- Harvested rainwater may be used for: toilet flushing, landscape irrigation, car washing, cooling tower make-up
- Storage tank sizing based on roof catchment area and local rainfall data

### Roof U-Value Requirements (2021 Amendment)

| Roof Type | Maximum U-Value |
|-----------|----------------|
| Light load roof (below 50 kg/sq m) | **0.4 W/m2K** |
| Heavy load roof (50 kg/sq m and above) | **0.6 W/m2K** |

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VI, By-Law 115; Amendment 2021

## By-Law 117: Roof Vault Access — 2021 Amendment

- Access openings to roof vaults/spaces must be minimum **600mm × 600mm**
- Access must be provided for maintenance and inspection purposes

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 117

## By-Law 124: Lifts

- Lift installations must comply with relevant Malaysian Standards
- Fire lifts required for buildings exceeding **4 storeys** or **18 metres** in height
- Lift shafts must be enclosed within fire-rated construction
- Emergency mode of operation required during mains power failure
- Fire mode of operation required for firefighter use
- Lift shaft ventilation and smoke detector requirements for lobbies

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VI, By-Law 124

## Materials and Workmanship

- All building materials must be sound, suitable for their intended purpose, and of adequate durability
- Timber used in permanent structures must be treated against termites and decay
- Concrete cover to reinforcement must be adequate for the exposure conditions
- Workmanship must be to a standard acceptable to the local authority

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VI

## Deleted By-Laws (2021 Amendment)

The following constructional by-laws were deleted or substantially replaced in 2021:
- Several by-laws relating to obsolete construction methods and materials were removed
- References updated from British Standards to Malaysian Standards (PM/MS) throughout Part VI
