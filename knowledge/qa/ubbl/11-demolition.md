# Part IA — Building Demolition

Part 1A was introduced by the 2021 Amendment, adding By-Laws 2a through 2g to regulate demolition works.

## By-Law 2a: Demolition Plan Required

- No person shall demolish any building without first submitting a **demolition plan** to the local authority
- The demolition plan must include:
  - Method statement for demolition
  - **Structural stability report** prepared by a Professional Engineer
  - Compliance with **PM 2318** (Malaysian Standard for demolition)
  - Safety measures for workers and public
  - Environmental controls (dust, noise, vibration)
  - Waste management and disposal plan

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 2a

## By-Law 2b: Approval Before Demolition

- Local authority may **approve, reject, or impose conditions** on the demolition plan
- Rejection grounds include inadequate safety measures, insufficient structural analysis, or environmental concerns
- Approval is valid for **12 months** from date of issue

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 2b

## By-Law 2c: Notice Requirements

- Written notice of **7 days** must be given to the local authority before commencement of demolition
- Notice must include the date of commencement and expected duration
- Adjacent property owners must be notified in writing

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 2c

## By-Law 2d: Supervision

- Demolition works must be supervised by a **Professional Engineer** or qualified person
- Supervisor must be present on site during critical demolition phases
- Daily records of demolition progress must be maintained

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 2d

## By-Law 2e: Safety Requirements

- Compliance with **PM 2318** (Code of Practice for Demolition of Buildings) is mandatory
- Hoarding and protective barriers required around demolition site
- Public access routes must be protected or diverted
- Structural propping of adjacent buildings where necessary

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 2e

## By-Law 2f: Exemptions

- The following are exempt from the full demolition plan requirements:
  - Minor demolition works (non-structural partitions, finishes)
  - Buildings not exceeding **2 storeys** where no adjacent structures are affected
- Exempted works still require notification to the local authority

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 2f

## By-Law 2g: Completion

- Upon completion of demolition, the site must be made safe and secured
- A completion notice must be submitted to the local authority
- The site must be cleared of debris and hazardous materials

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 2g
