# Accessibility Requirements (OKU)

## By-Law 34A: Application

- Accessibility requirements apply to all public buildings and buildings with public access (UBBL By-Law 34A(1))
- Compliance with **MS 1184:2014** (Code of Practice on Access for Disabled Persons to Public Buildings) is mandatory (UBBL By-Law 34A(2))
- Exemptions: individual private dwellings (Purpose Group I) (UBBL By-Law 34A(3))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IIIA, By-Law 34A

## By-Law 34B: Accessible Routes

- An accessible route shall be provided from the site entrance to the building entrance (UBBL By-Law 34B(1))
- Accessible route minimum width: **1.2 metres** (UBBL By-Law 34B(2))
- Surface shall be firm, stable, and slip-resistant (UBBL By-Law 34B(3))
- Level changes greater than **13mm** require ramped approach (UBBL By-Law 34B(4))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IIIA, By-Law 34B

## By-Law 34C: Ramps

- Maximum ramp gradient: **1:12** (8.33%) (UBBL By-Law 34C(1))
- Minimum ramp width: **1.2 metres** (UBBL By-Law 34C(2))
- Maximum ramp run without landing: **6.0 metres** (UBBL By-Law 34C(3))
- Landing length: minimum **1.5 metres** (UBBL By-Law 34C(4))
- Handrails required on both sides at **0.84-0.92 metres** height (UBBL By-Law 34C(5))
- Handrails shall extend **0.3 metres** beyond top and bottom of ramp (UBBL By-Law 34C(6))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IIIA, By-Law 34C

## By-Law 34D: Accessible Toilets

- At least **1 accessible toilet** per floor in public buildings (UBBL By-Law 34D(1))
- Minimum dimensions: **1.5m × 1.8m** clear floor space (UBBL By-Law 34D(2))
- Door width: minimum **0.9 metres**, outward-opening or sliding (UBBL By-Law 34D(3))
- Grab bars: horizontal bars at **0.8-0.85 metres** from finished floor level (UBBL By-Law 34D(4))
- WC seat height: **0.43-0.46 metres** from finished floor level (UBBL By-Law 34D(5))
- Emergency alarm pull cord to be reachable from floor level (UBBL By-Law 34D(6))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IIIA, By-Law 34D

## By-Law 34E: Lifts

- Where lifts are provided, at least **1 accessible lift** shall be available (UBBL By-Law 34E(1))
- Minimum lift car size: **1.1m × 1.4m** (UBBL By-Law 34E(2))
- Lift door opening: minimum **0.9 metres** (UBBL By-Law 34E(3))
- Control buttons: between **0.9m and 1.2m** above floor (UBBL By-Law 34E(4))
- Braille and tactile indicators required (UBBL By-Law 34E(5))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IIIA, By-Law 34E

## By-Law 34F: Signage

- Signage with the international symbol of access shall be provided at all accessible facilities (UBBL By-Law 34F(1))
- Tactile ground surface indicators (TGSI) at hazardous locations (stairs, ramps, pedestrian crossings) (UBBL By-Law 34F(2))
- Wayfinding signage: minimum character height **15mm** per metre of viewing distance (UBBL By-Law 34F(3))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part IIIA, By-Law 34F
