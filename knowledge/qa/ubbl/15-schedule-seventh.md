# Seventh Schedule — Travel Distance and Occupant Load

Referenced by By-laws 165(4), 166(2), 170(b), 174(1), 174A(1)(b), 168(2), 175, 176, 177, 181.

| Purpose Group | Dead-end (m) | Unsprinkled 2-way (m) | Sprinkled 2-way (m) | Corridor Dead-end (m) |
|---|---|---|---|---|

| **VII** Place of Assembly | **15** | **45** | **60** | **15** |
| **VIII** Storage (low/ordinary) | **15** | **30** | **60** | **15** |
| VIII Storage (high hazard) | **10** | **20** | **35** | **10** |
| VIII Parking garage | **15** | 45+ | 60× | **15** |
| VIII Aircraft hangar (ground) | **15** | 30+ | 45+ | **15** |
| VIII Aircraft hangar (mezzanine) | **15** | **20** | **20** | **15** |

**NR** = No requirement. **×** = sprinkled garage below street limited to 45m. **+** = See aircraft hangar special provisions.

**Key notes:**
- Dead-end limit is measured to a storey exit or to a point where an alternative escape route is available
- Total travel distance shall not exceed the limit under the two-way column
- In an open plan, the direct distance shall be **two-thirds** of the travel distance
- Aircraft hangars: where undivided floor area exceeds 45m to nearest exit, staircase to exit tunnel or overhead passageway may satisfy requirement; maximum travel in any case shall not exceed **120 metres**

> **Citation:** Seventh Schedule, UBBL 1984 (Amendment 2012/2021)

**Key notes:**
