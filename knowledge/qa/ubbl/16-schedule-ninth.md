# Ninth Schedule — Fire Resistance Periods

Referenced by By-laws 142(3), 147, 158(1), 162, 213, 216(2).

## Definitions

- **"cubic capacity"** = cubic capacity of the building or compartment
- **"floor area"** = floor area of each storey in the building or compartment
- **"height"** = as defined in By-law 215(2)
- **"NL"** = no limit applicable

**Key notes:**
- Higher fire resistance periods apply to basements throughout
- Where sprinklers are installed, compartment dimensions may be doubled
- Buildings over **30 metres**: all floors must be compartment floors (By-law 136)
- The fire resistance period applies to all elements of structure including walls, floors, columns, beams, and structural frames
