# Fire Requirements

## By-Law 133: Fire Resistance Requirements

Minimum fire resistance periods by building purpose group:

| Building Group | Height ≤ 18m | Height > 18m |
|---------------|-------------|-------------|
| I (Small residential) | 1 hour | N/A |
| II (Large residential) | 1 hour | 2 hours |
| III (Institutional) | 1 hour | 2 hours |
| IV (Office) | 1 hour | 2 hours |
| V (Shop/commercial) | 1 hour | 2 hours |
| VI (Factory) | 1 hour | 2 hours |
| VII (Assembly) | 1 hour | 2 hours |

- Elements of structure (columns, beams, floors, load-bearing walls) shall have the specified fire resistance (UBBL By-Law 133(1))
- Basements: minimum **2 hours** fire resistance regardless of building height (UBBL By-Law 133(2))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 133

## By-Law 136: Compartmentation

- Maximum compartment size for non-sprinklered buildings:
  - Office/residential: **2,000 sq metres** per floor
  - Shop/commercial: **1,000 sq metres** per floor
  - Factory/warehouse: **2,000 sq metres** per floor
- With sprinkler system: compartment size may be **doubled** (UBBL By-Law 136(2))
- Compartment walls shall have fire resistance not less than the structural elements (UBBL By-Law 136(3))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 136

## By-Law 140: Fire Doors

- Fire doors to have fire resistance of not less than **half** the required wall/floor resistance (UBBL By-Law 140(1))
- Minimum fire door rating: **30 minutes** (UBBL By-Law 140(2))
- Fire doors shall be self-closing (UBBL By-Law 140(3))
- Fire doors opening onto escape routes shall swing in the direction of travel (UBBL By-Law 140(4))
- Maximum gap under fire door: **10mm** (UBBL By-Law 140(5))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 140

## By-Law 145: Escape Routes

- Every building shall have adequate escape routes to enable safe evacuation (UBBL By-Law 145(1))
- Maximum travel distance to nearest exit:
  - With sprinklers: **45 metres**
  - Without sprinklers: **30 metres**
- Dead-end travel distance maximum: **15 metres** (UBBL By-Law 145(3))
- Minimum number of exits: **2** for any floor with occupant load > 50 (UBBL By-Law 145(4))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 145

## By-Law 148: Exit Widths

- Minimum exit width: **0.85 metres** per unit of exit (UBBL By-Law 148(1))
- Exit width calculation: 1 unit per **40 persons** for stairs, 1 unit per **60 persons** for level exits (UBBL By-Law 148(2))
- No exit shall be less than **1.0 metre** wide (UBBL By-Law 148(3))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 148

## By-Law 153: Fire Lifts

- Fire lifts required for buildings exceeding **4 storeys** or **18 metres** in height (UBBL By-Law 153(1))
- Fire lift lobby shall be a protected lobby with fire resistance not less than **2 hours** (UBBL By-Law 153(2))
- Fire lift shall have minimum car size: **1.1m × 2.1m** (UBBL By-Law 153(3))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 153
