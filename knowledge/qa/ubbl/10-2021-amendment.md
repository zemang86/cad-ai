# UBBL 2021 Amendment — Comprehensive Summary

The 2021 Amendment to the Uniform Building By-Laws 1984 is the most significant revision since the original enactment. It introduces new Parts, new by-laws, deletes obsolete provisions, and updates standards references throughout.

## Part 1A: Building Demolition (NEW)

- **By-Laws 2a–2g**: New Part regulating demolition works
- Demolition plan with structural stability report per **PM 2318** required
- Local authority approval, 7-day notice, PE supervision mandatory
- See `ubbl/11-demolition.md` for full details

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), Part 1A

## Part I: Definitions and General (Amended)

- **By-Law 2**: New definitions added — "principal submitting person", "contractor", "construction project manager", "SPAH", "disabled person"
- **By-Law 2**: "Smoke door" definition **deleted**
- **By-Law 5**: Construction project manager and site supervisor now required alongside PSP
- **By-Law 8**: Plans copies changed from "triplicate" to "as the local authority may determine"
- **By-Law 10**: SPAH (rainwater harvesting) required for all buildings with **roof area ≥100 sq m**
- **By-Law 25**: CCC process overhauled — 20 paragraphs, Forms G1–G21, multi-agency sign-off (JBPM, DOSH, TNB, IWK, JKR)

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), Part I

## Part III: Space, Light and Ventilation (Amended)

- **By-Law 37(4)**: Measurement changed from 1.83m to **1.80 metres**
- **By-Law 38(1)**: Measurement changed from 2.25m to **2.10 metres**
- **By-Law 38a**: Energy efficiency — non-residential >4,000 sq m must comply with **MS 1525**, OTTV ≤**50 W/m2**, RTTV ≤**25 W/m2**
- **By-Law 38ba**: Lightning protection per **MS IEC 62305** (new)
- **By-Law 39**: Ventilation percentages reviewed; mechanical ventilation provisions added
- **By-Law 99**: Kitchen exhaust ducts — refractory material, 2-hour fire rating, must reach roof
- **By-Law 109**: Spiral staircases **banned from means of escape**

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), Part III

## Part V: Structural (Amended)

- **By-Laws 54–69**: All structural load references migrated from BS to **PM PE 1991** (Eurocode 1) series
- **By-Law 71**: Vehicle barriers now reference **PM PE 1991-1-1**
- **By-Law 73(1a)**: Geotechnical field + laboratory tests required (new)
- **By-Law 76a**: Buildings on hillside — additional structural submissions (new)
- **By-Laws 74, 76, 77**: **Deleted** (superseded by PM PE 1991 series)

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), Part V

## Part VI: Constructional (Amended)

- **By-Law 86**: Party walls — **STC 50** acoustic requirement, ≥200mm stone or ≥100mm RC (replaced)
- **By-Law 106/107**: Room dimension changes aligned with By-Law 42
- **By-Law 115**: SPAH expanded — green pipes, "No For Drinks" marking, cross-contamination prevention, backflow devices
- **By-Law 117**: Roof vault access — 600mm minimum opening

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), Part VI

## Part VII: Fire Safety (Major Amendments)

- **By-Law 136a**: Fire shutters — PM 1073 compliance, activation methods, signage (new, 7 paragraphs)
- **By-Law 138a**: Partition walls — Ninth Schedule compliance (new)
- **By-Law 139**: Fire risk area isolation — detailed list including boiler rooms, LPG storage, transformer rooms, hospital labs
- **By-Law 140**: Fire appliance access — **completely replaced** (6m width, 30-ton load, ≤1:15 gradient, 4.5m clearance)
- **By-Law 144**: External wall coatings — <2m from boundary = non-combustible per PB 8414
- **By-Law 172**: Emergency exit signs updated to **MS 2687** and **MS 619**
- **By-Law 174a**: Final exit definition — must discharge to street/open space (new)
- **By-Law 194**: Single staircase — **replaced** (≤12m OR ≤2 storeys with first floor ≤6m)
- **By-Law 197a**: Fire-fighting holes — required >18m height (new)
- **By-Law 197b**: Fire-fighting access space — 6 sq m per floor at fire lift lobby (new)
- **By-Law 202**: Stair pressurization threshold changed from >30m to **>45m**
- **By-Law 211a**: Plastic materials **prohibited** in walls, ceilings, roofs (new)
- **By-Law 224A**: Hospital fire safety — 9 subsections (new, see building-types/03-healthcare.md)

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), Part VII

## Part VIII: Fire Fighting Systems (Amended)

- **By-Law 225**: Fire hydrant distances — ≥30m from entrance, ≤90m apart, per PM 1489
- **By-Law 226a**: Hose loop system per PM 1489 (new)
- **By-Law 228a**: Automatic fire surveillance system (new)
- **By-Law 230**: Dry riser — >18m but <30m, per PM 1489
- **By-Law 231**: Wet riser — >30m, per PM 1489, 150m stages, ≤20 bar pressure

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), Part VIII

## Schedules (Amended/New)

- **Schedule 11**: Staircase landings — new schedule specifying minimum landing dimensions
- **By-Law 257**: Malaysian Standard prevails over British Standard (reinforced)

## Roof U-Value Requirements (All Buildings)

| Roof Type | Maximum U-Value |
|-----------|----------------|
| Light load roof (below 50 kg/sq m) | **0.4 W/m2K** |
| Heavy load roof (50 kg/sq m and above) | **0.6 W/m2K** |

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021)
