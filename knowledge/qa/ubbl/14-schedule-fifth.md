# Fifth Schedule — Purpose Groups and Building/Compartment Dimensions

Referenced by By-laws 134 and 138.

## Designation of Purpose Groups

| Purpose Group | Title | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **I** | Small Residential | Private dwelling house — detached, semi-detached, or terraced |
| **II** | Institutional | Hospital, school, college, library, nursing home, or similar; persons suffering from disabilities, illness, old age, or under 5 years who sleep on premises |
| **III** | Other Residential | Accommodation not in Groups I or II — hotel, hostel, dormitory, apartment, flat, old folks' home, orphanage, service apartment |
| **IV** | Office | Premises used for administration, clerical work, handling money, telephone/telegraph operating |
| **V** | Shop | Retail trade, food court, wet/dry market, barber/hairdresser, repair services, shopping complex |
| **VI** | Factory | All premises under Factories and Machinery Act 1967, excluding Purpose Group VIII |
| **VII** | Place of Assembly | Social, recreational, educational, business activities — convention centre, museum, cinema, theatre, place of worship, transport terminal |
| **VIII** | Storage and General | Storage, deposit or parking of goods, materials (including vehicles); premises not in Groups I–VII |

> **Citation:** Fifth Schedule, UBBL 1984 (Amendment 2012/2021)

## Dimensions of Buildings and Compartments (By-law 136)

### Part 1 — Multi-Storey Buildings

| Purpose Group | Height | Max Floor Area (m²) | Max Cubic Capacity (m³) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **II** Institutional | Any | **2,000** | No limit |
| II Hospital (patient ward) | Any | **750** | No limit |
| **III** Other Residential | ≤28m | **3,000** | 8,500 |
| III Other Residential | >28m | **2,000** | 5,500 |
| **V** Shop | Any | **2,000** | 7,000 |
| **VI** Factory | ≤28m | No limit | **28,000** |
| VI Factory | >28m | **2,000** | 5,500 |
| **VIII** Storage & General | ≤28m | No limit | **21,000** |
| VIII Storage & General | >28m | **1,000** | No limit |

### Part 2 — Single Storey Buildings

| Purpose Group | Height | Max Floor Area (m²) | Max Cubic Capacity (m³) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **II** Institutional | Any | **3,000** | No limit |
| II Hospital (patient ward) | Any | **750** | No limit |
| **III** Other Residential | Any | **3,000** | No limit |

**Notes:**
- Purpose Groups **I, IV, and VII** are excluded — no dimension limits applicable under By-law 138
- Single storey hospital patient wards without sprinklers: each ward must be a compartment with **1-hour fire resistance** and **½-hour fire resistance doors**; automatic fire alarm required
- Where sprinkler systems are installed, compartment size limits may be **doubled** (By-law 136)

> **Citation:** Fifth Schedule, UBBL 1984 (Amendment 2021)
