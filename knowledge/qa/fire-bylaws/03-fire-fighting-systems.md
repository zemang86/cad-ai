# Fire Fighting Systems

## Hose Reel System

- Hose: **30 metres** long, **25mm** wide, rubber construction
- Nozzle: adjustable, **8mm** recommended
- Supply pipes: minimum **25mm**, strong metal, **painted red**
- Coverage: installed **within 30 metres** of all areas requiring protection
- For Class A fires (wood, paper, textiles, rubber, plastics)

> **Citation**: Fire Services Act 1988; BOMBA requirements

## Fire Hydrant System

- **Two pump sets** required (one duty, one standby)
- Capacity: **3,000 litres/min** at **4-7 bars** running pressure to 3 hydrants simultaneously
- Backup diesel/power generator for minimum **2 hours**
- Water tank minimum capacity: **180,000 litres** (1-hour supply for 3 hydrants)
- Auto-refill at **20 litres/second**
- Internal hydrants at every floor landing in buildings requiring hydrant systems

### By-Law 225: Hydrant Distances — 2021 Amendment

- External hydrant must be located **≥30 metres** from the building entrance (to prevent fire damage to supply)
- Maximum spacing between hydrants: **≤90 metres** apart
- Design and installation per **PM 1489** (Malaysian Standard for fire hydrant systems)

> **Citation**: Fire Services Act 1988; UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 225; PM 1489

## Sprinkler System (MS 1910:2017)

- Automatic detection, control, extinguishment, and occupant warning
- Components: pumps, water tanks, control valve sets, sprinkler heads, flow switches, pressure switches, pipework, valves
- Glass bulb breaks at set temperature, releasing water
- Design per **MS 1910:2017** and **NFPA 13**
- ESFR (Early Suppression Fast Response) sprinklers for atriums and large void areas
- With sprinklers: compartment size limits **doubled (x2)** per UBBL By-Law 136

> **Citation**: MS 1910:2017; UBBL Part VIII

## Fire Alarm and Detection System

- Smoke detectors, heat detectors, manual call points required
- Audible alarms and visual strobes at every floor
- Main fire alarm control panel at ground floor or fire command centre
- **SPKA** connection to nearest fire station mandatory for designated buildings
- Backup lighting: battery-operated minimum **1 hour**, along corridors, exits, staircases
- Emergency lighting duration: minimum **90 minutes** (UBBL By-Law 160(3))

> **Citation**: UBBL Part VIII; Fire Services Act 1988

## Dry Riser System

- Required for buildings where topmost floor is **>18 metres** and **<30 metres** above fire appliance access level (2021 Amendment — previously >18.3m and <30.5m)
- Design and installation per **PM 1489**
- Breeching inlets at ground level for fire brigade connection
- Outlet valves at every floor for firefighter use

> **Citation**: UBBL Part VIII; UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 230; PM 1489

## Wet Riser System

- Required for buildings where topmost floor is **>30 metres** above fire appliance access level (2021 Amendment — previously >30.5m)
- Design and installation per **PM 1489**
- System divided into stages of maximum **150 metres** vertical height
- Maximum working pressure: **≤20 bars** at any outlet
- Permanently charged with water under pressure
- Pump sets and water storage as per fire hydrant requirements

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 231; PM 1489

## By-Law 226a: Hose Loop System — 2021 Amendment (NEW)

- Hose loop system required in designated buildings per **PM 1489**
- Provides continuous water supply loop for fire-fighting operations
- Connected to the building's fire hydrant/riser system

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 226a; PM 1489

## By-Law 228a: Automatic Fire Surveillance System — 2021 Amendment (NEW)

- Automatic fire surveillance system required in designated high-risk buildings
- System must provide real-time monitoring and alarm activation
- Connected to fire alarm panel and SPKA where required

> **Citation**: UBBL (Amendment 2021), By-Law 228a

## Emergency Power

- Fire fighting systems must have power supply independent of main electrical system
- Minimum **2 hours** operation on emergency power
- Emergency generator must start automatically upon mains failure
- Must serve: fire lifts, emergency lighting, fire pumps, smoke control systems, fire alarm systems

> **Citation**: UBBL Part VIII; BOMBA requirements
