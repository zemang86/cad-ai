# Fire Escape and Egress Requirements

## Travel Distance

Maximum travel distance to the nearest exit:

| Condition | Distance |
|-----------|----------|
| With sprinkler system | **45 metres** |
| Without sprinkler system | **30 metres** |
| Dead-end corridor | **15 metres** |
| Dead-end (with sprinkler) | **22.5 metres** |

- Travel distance is measured along the line of travel (UBBL By-Law 145(2))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 145

## Exit Widths

- Minimum exit door width: **1.0 metre** (UBBL By-Law 148(3))
- Exit width calculation based on occupant load:
  - **Stairs**: 1 unit (0.85m) per 40 persons
  - **Level exits**: 1 unit (0.85m) per 60 persons
- Minimum number of exits: **2** for any floor with occupant load exceeding 50 persons (UBBL By-Law 145(4))
- Exits shall be spaced so that the distance between any two exits is not less than **half the diagonal** of the floor area (UBBL By-Law 145(5))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 148

## Protected Staircases

- Escape staircases shall be enclosed in fire-rated construction (UBBL By-Law 150(1))
- Fire rating of staircase enclosure: not less than fire resistance of the building structure (UBBL By-Law 150(2))
- Minimum staircase width for escape: **1.05 metres** (UBBL By-Law 150(3))
- No combustible materials within escape staircases (UBBL By-Law 150(4))
- Smoke ventilation: minimum **1.0 sq metre** openable vent at top of staircase, or pressurization system (UBBL By-Law 150(5))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 150

## Emergency Lighting

- Emergency lighting required in all escape routes, exit passages, and staircases (UBBL By-Law 160(1))
- Minimum illumination level: **1 lux** at floor level along centre line of escape route (UBBL By-Law 160(2))
- Emergency lighting duration: minimum **90 minutes** (UBBL By-Law 160(3))
- Exit signs: illuminated, minimum **200mm** character height, visible from **30 metres** (UBBL By-Law 160(4))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 160

## Occupant Load Calculation

Occupant load factors for calculating exit requirements:

| Use | Floor Area per Person |
|-----|----------------------|
| Assembly (standing) | **0.5 sq metres** |
| Assembly (seated) | **1.0 sq metres** |
| Office | **6.0 sq metres** |
| Shop/retail | **3.0 sq metres** |
| Residential | **10.0 sq metres** |
| Factory | **10.0 sq metres** |
| Storage/warehouse | **30.0 sq metres** |

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, Schedule to By-Law 145
