# Fire Door Ratings and Requirements

## Fire Door Rating Categories

| Rating | Fire Resistance | Typical Application |
|--------|----------------|-------------------|
| Half-hour door | **30 minutes** | Minimum rating for any fire door |
| One-hour door | **75 minutes** | Compartment walls with 1-hour fire resistance |
| Two-hour door | **135 minutes** | Compartment walls with 2-hour fire resistance |

- Fire doors must have fire resistance of not less than **half** the required wall/floor fire resistance (By-Law 140(1))
- Minimum fire door rating in any application: **30 minutes** (By-Law 140(2))

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 140; By-Law 163

## Self-Closing Requirement

- All fire doors shall be **self-closing** (By-Law 140(3))
- Door closers must comply with relevant Malaysian Standards
- Hold-open devices permitted only if connected to fire alarm system (automatic release upon alarm)
- Fire doors opening onto escape routes shall swing in the **direction of travel**

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 140, By-Law 164

## Gap and Seal Requirements

- Maximum gap under fire door: **10mm** (By-Law 140(5))
- Intumescent seals required around door frames
- Smoke seals recommended for doors in smoke-control zones
- Door frames must have same fire rating as the door leaf

> **Citation**: Uniform Building By-Laws 1984, Part VII, By-Law 140

## Testing Standard

- Fire door testing per **MS 1073: Part 3: 1996 (Amd.1: 2003)** -- Specification for methods of determination of fire resistance
- Testing determines integrity, insulation, and stability under fire conditions
- Testing conducted at accredited laboratories

> **Citation**: MS 1073: Part 3: 1996 (Amd.1: 2003)

## BOMBA Certification (SIRIM)

- All fire doors require **Sijil Perakuan Bahan BOMBA** (BOMBA Material Certification)
- Certification issued via **SIRIM QAS International**
- BOMBA directive: **Arahan Keselamatan Kebakaran Bilangan 2 Tahun 2012**
- Only BOMBA-certified fire doors may be used in designated premises
- Regular inspection of fire doors required as condition of Fire Certificate

> **Citation**: BOMBA Arahan Keselamatan Kebakaran Bil. 2/2012; SIRIM QAS
