# Water Supply and Sewerage

## IWK/SPAN Sewerage Approval

IWK appointed by SPAN as Certifying Agency under Section 174 of the Water Services Industry Act 2006 (Act 655).

### Simplified Approval Model (Effective 1 April 2014)

| Process | Duration |
|---------|----------|
| Sewerage Works Approval (SWA) | **14 days** |
| Sewerage Works Completion (SWC) Certification Notice | **7 days** |
| **Total charter** | **21 days** |

(Down from 63 days under previous model)

> **Citation**: SPAN/IWK Sewerage Approval Guidelines

## Contractor Requirements

- Contractors must be registered with **CIDB**
- Must hold **SPAN Type-C (Pembetungan)** license for sewerage construction
- M&E works require **SPAN Type-D (Pembetungan)** license

## MSIG (Malaysian Sewerage Industry Guidelines)

- Published by SPAN
- Governs planning, design, and construction of sewerage systems
- Developments should **connect to existing sewage treatment plants** if capacity allows, rather than constructing new smaller systems

## Water Supply

- SPAN publishes the **Uniform Technical Guidelines (UTG)** for water supply infrastructure
- Water supply connection coordinated through respective state water authorities

> **Citation**: Water Services Industry Act 2006 (Act 655); SPAN MSIG
