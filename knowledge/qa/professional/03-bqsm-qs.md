# BQSM (Board of Quantity Surveyors Malaysia)

## Quantity Surveyors Act 1967

- Established under the **Quantity Surveyors Act 1967**, under the Ministry of Works (KKR)
- BQSM keeps register of firms and bodies corporate practising as consulting quantity surveyors
- Fixes the scales of fees for consulting QS services (with Minister of Public Works approval)

> **Citation**: Quantity Surveyors Act 1967

## Assessment of Professional Competence (APC)

- Registration requires **APC**: supervised professional work with diary and log books, critical analysis reporting, and professional interview
- Examination registration requires at least **1 year** of work experience under a registered BQSM consulting firm or public agency
- Applications processed within **4 months** from receipt

> **Citation**: BQSM APC Guidelines
