# BEM (Board of Engineers Malaysia) -- Engineers

## Registration Pathway

1. Register as **Graduate Engineer** (mandatory under Section 7(2)(a) of Act 138) with BEM-recognized or Washington Accord-accredited degree
2. Gain at least **3 years** of practical experience after registration
3. Pass the **Professional Assessment Examination (PAE)** administered by IEM
4. Pass the **Professional Competency Examination (PCE)** to obtain Practising Certificate (PEPC)
   - Part A: Non-technical (laws, ethics, public responsibility)
   - Part B: Technical (Civil, Electrical, Mechanical, Chemical, Environmental, Mining, Geotechnical)

> **Citation**: Registration of Engineers Act 1967 (Act 138)

## Registration Categories

- Professional Engineers with Practising Certificate (PEPC)
- Professional Engineers (PE)
- Accredited Checkers (Structural)
- Accredited Checkers (Geotechnical)
- Graduate Engineers
- Engineering Technologists
- Inspectors of Works

## Key Requirements

- A PE **without** a Practising Certificate cannot submit plans to authorities
- Only a **PEPC** can sign the Certificate of Completion and Compliance (CCC)
- The submitting engineer must specify and supervise subsurface investigation for structural design
- PEPC engaged by contractor is responsible for designing, endorsing, and supervising temporary works

> **Citation**: Registration of Engineers Act 1967 (Act 138)
