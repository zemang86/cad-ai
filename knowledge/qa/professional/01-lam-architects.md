# LAM (Board of Architects Malaysia) -- Architects

## Architects Act 1967 (Act 117)

- Only registered architects may use the title "Architect" or the honorific "Ar." -- protected by law
- Board of Architects Malaysia consists of **18 members**

## Scale of Minimum Fees

Fees range from **3.25% to 10%** of the project's final construction cost, decreasing as cost increases.

Three building categories:
- **Category 1**: Complex buildings
- **Category 2**: Moderate complexity
- **Category 3**: Simple buildings

### Fee Disbursement by Phase

| Phase | Percentage |
|-------|-----------|
| Schematic Design | **15%** |
| Design Development | **30%** |
| Contract Documentation | **25%** |
| Contract Implementation & Management | **25%** |
| Final Completion | **5%** |

> **Citation**: Architects (Scale of Minimum Fees) Rules 2010

## Principal Submitting Person (PSP)

- Architects serve as the **PSP** under UBBL provisions
- Responsible for project submissions, obtaining approvals, and periodic supervision during construction
- Under CCC system: PSP self-certifies compliance (replaces local authority certification)

> **Citation**: Architects Act 1967; UBBL Part II

## Practice Requirements

- **Sole proprietorship**: sole proprietor must be a Professional Architect
- **Partnership**: all partners must be Professional Architects
- **Body corporate**: board of 2+ Professional Architects, shares held by Professional Architects, minimum paid-up capital **RM50,000**
- **Professional indemnity insurance** required
- LAM has power to impose penalties from fines to suspension or revocation

> **Citation**: Architects Act 1967 (Act 117)
