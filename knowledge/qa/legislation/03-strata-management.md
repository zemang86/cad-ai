# Strata Titles and Management

## Strata Titles Act 1985 (Act 318)

Applies to Peninsular Malaysia, Federal Territory of Putrajaya, and Federal Territory of Labuan.

| Provision | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Eligibility for subdivision | Buildings with **2 or more storeys** on alienated land under final title |
| Land subdivision | Land with **2 or more buildings** on one lot |
| Compulsory application | **3 months** from date of sale/agreement to sell any parcel |
| Strata plan application | Within **3 months** using **Form 10** |
| Phased developments | Section 10A provides for phased subdivision |

> **Citation**: Strata Titles Act 1985 (Act 318)

## Strata Management Act 2013 (Act 757)

Came into force **1 June 2015**, repealing the Building and Common Property (Maintenance and Management) Act 2007.

### Management Phases

| Phase | Body | Timeline |
|-------|------|----------|
| Developer Management | Developer | From vacant possession until JMB formation |
| Joint Management Body (JMB) | Developer + Purchasers | First AGM within **12 months** of vacant possession OR when **1/3 of units sold** |
| Management Corporation (MC) | Parcel owners only | Upon registration of strata titles |

### Developer Obligations

- Maintain common property until JMB formation
- Open and maintain maintenance and sinking fund accounts
- Convene first AGM within prescribed timeline
- Hand over documents (building plans, financial records) to JMB/MC within **30 days** of formation

> **Citation**: Strata Management Act 2013 (Act 757)

### Financial Framework

| Account | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Maintenance Charges Account** | General expenditure: cleaning, security, insurance, repairs, legal fees |
| **Sinking Fund Account** | Capital expenditure: painting, refurbishment, major works |

- Maintenance charges imposed in proportion to **share units** of each parcel
- Single, consistent rate for all parcels

### Strata Management Tribunal (SMT)

- Jurisdiction over disputes related to strata management
- Disputes regarding failure to perform duties under SMA 2013

> **Citation**: Strata Management Act 2013 (Act 757)
