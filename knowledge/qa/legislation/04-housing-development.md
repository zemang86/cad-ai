# Housing Development (Control and Licensing) Act 1966

Applies to **Peninsular Malaysia** only.

## Developer Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Licensing | **APDL** (Advertising Permit and Developer's License) required from KPKT |
| Deposit | **3%** of estimated construction cost |
| Housing Development Account | Mandatory account with licensed financial institution; all purchaser funds deposited here |
| Separate accounts | Required for each housing development project |
| Directors | At least **2 directors** must be Malaysian residents |
| Advertising | Must include developer name/address, expected completion date, unit types |

> **Citation**: Housing Development (Control and Licensing) Act 1966 (Act 118)

## Purchaser Protections

| Protection | Details |
|------------|---------|
| Defect Liability Period | **24 months** from receipt of keys |
| Right to terminate | If developer refuses/delays/suspends/ceases work for **6 continuous months** or more after SPA execution |
| Dispute resolution | **Homebuyer Tribunal** established under the Act |

> **Citation**: Housing Development (Control and Licensing) Act 1966 (Act 118)

## Oversight Bodies

- **Housing Controller** -- oversight and enforcement
- **Tribunal for Homebuyer Claims** -- dispute resolution for purchasers
