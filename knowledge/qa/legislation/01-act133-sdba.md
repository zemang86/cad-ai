# Street, Drainage and Building Act 1974 (Act 133)

The parent Act under which the UBBL is made. Applies to **Peninsular Malaysia** only.

## Overview

- Primary legislation governing streets, drainage, and building control in Peninsular Malaysia
- UBBL 1984 is delegated legislation made under Section 133 of this Act
- Administered by local authorities (PBTs) under supervision of KPKT

## Key Provisions

| Section | Subject | Details |
|---------|---------|---------|
| 10 | Expiry of approved plans | Plans expire **1 year** from approval; renewal max **12 months** |
| 12-13 | Appeal process | Appeal within **7 days** of disapproval; if no response in **2 months**, may appeal to State Authority |
| 70B | Erecting a building | Includes new building, additions, alterations, conversions, departures from approved plans |
| 70C | Revocation of approval | Local authority may revoke approval upon conviction under s.70B(15) |
| 70D | Inspection at any stage | Local authority may inspect at any stage and notify of deviations |

> **Citation**: Street, Drainage and Building Act 1974, Sections 10-70D

## Penalties

| Offence | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| Building erection offences (s.70B) | Fine up to **RM100,000** or imprisonment up to **5 years**, or both; continuing **RM500/day** |
| Earthworks/drainage contravention | Fine up to **RM50,000** or imprisonment up to **5 years**, or both; continuing **RM500/day** |
| Dangerous/defective private streets | Fine up to **RM5,000** or imprisonment up to **12 months**, or both |
| Street/culvert not per approved plans | Fine up to **RM2,000** |
| Contrary to prohibition/closing order | Fine up to **RM100/day** |

> **Citation**: Street, Drainage and Building Act 1974, various sections

## Local Authority Powers

- Monitor compliance, conduct inspections at any construction stage
- Issue **stop-work orders** for violations
- Order repairs or demolition of dangerous/non-compliant structures
- Charge owners for costs of demolition carried out by authority
- Revoke building approval upon conviction
- Require professional supervision of building works

> **Citation**: Street, Drainage and Building Act 1974

## CCC Amendment (2007)

- Section 70 amended to introduce the **Certificate of Completion and Compliance (CCC)** system
- CCC replaced the CFO (Certificate of Fitness for Occupation)
- PSP (Principal Submitting Person) self-certifies compliance
- Local authority no longer issues occupancy certificates

> **Citation**: Street, Drainage and Building Act 1974 (Amendment 2007), Section 70
