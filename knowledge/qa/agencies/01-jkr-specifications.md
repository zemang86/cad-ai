## Road Works Specification (JKR/SPJ/2015-S1)

- Document reference: **JKR 20401-0051-15**
- Covers programme of works, site limitations, supply of materials, setting out, dimensions, levels, inspections and tests
- Section 3: Drainage works
- Section 6: Pavement works
- Section 9: Concrete works

> **Citation**: JKR Standard Specification for Road Works, JKR/SPJ/2015-S1

## Standards Compliance (MS Equivalence Rule)

- Where a **Malaysian Standard (MS)** exists and is equivalent to a British or other specified standard, the MS shall be followed
- Other equivalent standards specifying superior material may be used with Superintending Officer (S.O.) approval
- This aligns with UBBL By-Law 257: Malaysian Standard prevails over British Standard

> **Citation**: JKR Standard Specifications; UBBL By-Law 257

## Geotechnical Design Guidelines

- JKR publishes geotechnical design guidelines for government projects
- Covers soil investigation, foundation design, slope stability
- For stormwater/drainage: refer to **MSMA (Urban Stormwater Management Manual for Malaysia) 2nd Edition** by JPS/DID

> **Citation**: JKR Geotechnical Design Guidelines
