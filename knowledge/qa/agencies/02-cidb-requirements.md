# CIDB (Construction Industry Development Board) Requirements

## Contractor Registration (Section 25, Act 520)

- All contractors must register with CIDB before undertaking construction work
- Minimum **70%** local equity (foreign equity max 30%; ASEAN foreign equity max 51%)
- Registration graded by contractor's ability and capability
- Certificate validity: **1 to 3 years**
- **Continuous Contractor Development (CCD)**: **40 CCD points/year** required for renewal

> **Citation**: Construction Industry Development Board Act 1994 (Act 520), Section 25

## Project Declaration (Section 34(1))

- Every awarded contract must be declared within **14 days** of award or before project starts
- Declaration via CIMS (Construction Industry Management System)

> **Citation**: CIDB Act 520, Section 34(1)

## Construction Levy (Section 34(2))

- Projects valued at **>RM500,000**: levy of **0.125% to 0.25%** of contract value
- Must be paid before work commences
- **Exemptions**: Low/medium cost housing (selling price <=RM100,000/unit); housing using >50% IBS components

> **Citation**: CIDB Act 520, Section 34(2)

## Construction Material Standards

- Materials (cement, iron/steel, ready-mix concrete) must be **CIDB-certified** before use on site

## Penalties

| Offence | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| Construction without valid registration | Fine **RM10,000 - RM100,000** |
| Non-declaration of projects | Fine up to **RM50,000** |
| Failure to pay levy | Fine up to **RM50,000** or **4x the levy** (whichever higher) |
| Unregistered construction personnel | Fine up to **RM5,000** |
| Uncertified construction materials | Fine **RM10,000 - RM500,000** |

> **Citation**: CIDB Act 520

## QLASSIC (Quality Assessment System in Construction)

Assessment standard: **CIS 7:2021**

| Score | Rating |
|-------|--------|
| **80%+** | Excellent |
| 70-79% | Good |
| 60-69% | Satisfactory |
| <60% | Needs improvement |

- Government projects costing **>RM10 million**: QLASSIC assessment mandatory
- Assessment covers architectural works, basic M&E fittings, external works
- First-time inspection only -- rectified works not re-assessed

> **Citation**: CIS 7:2021 (CIDB QLASSIC Standard)

## IBS (Industrialised Building System)

Assessment standard: **CIS 18:2023**

| Project Type | Threshold | Minimum IBS Score |
|-------------|-----------|-------------------|
| Government projects | Contract value >= **RM10 million** | **70 points** |
| Private projects | GFA >= **50,000 sq m** AND value >= **RM50 million** | **70 points** |

- IBS Score components: Structural System (max 50 pts), Wall System (max 20 pts), Other (max 30 pts)
- Levy exemption for projects using >50% IBS (apply before 40% structural progress)

> **Citation**: CIS 18:2023 (CIDB IBS Standard)
