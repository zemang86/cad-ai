# Educational Facilities (Schools)

## MOE Guidelines

### Space Standards

| Parameter | Requirement |
|-----------|------------|
| Minimum classroom size | **7m x 8m** (56 sq m) |
| Maximum students per classroom | 56 students |
| Administrative space | **5 sq m/person** |
| Air space per person (admin) | **12 cubic metres** |

> **Citation**: MOE Pekeliling dan Garis Panduan

### Site Size

| School Type | Minimum Site |
|-------------|-------------|
| Small primary school | **0.5 hectares** |
| Large secondary school | up to **5 hectares** |

- Ideal school locations: far from undesirable establishments, level accessible land
- Specifications cover window sizes, ceiling heights, floor thickness, roof slopes, corridor widths, and stair dimensions

> **Citation**: MOE Guidelines; PLANMalaysia Planning Guidelines

## High-Rise Schools

- New concept in Malaysia for areas with land scarcity (e.g., urban Kuala Lumpur)
- Multi-storey school buildings within high-density residential areas
- Must comply with all UBBL fire safety requirements for buildings of their height
- Additional considerations for child safety in vertical circulation

> **Citation**: MOE; PLANMalaysia
