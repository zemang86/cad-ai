# Solar and Renewable Energy

## Net Energy Metering (NEM) Scheme

| Category | Capacity Allocation |
|----------|-------------------|
| Residential | **600 MW** |
| Commercial-Industrial | **1,700 MW** |

- NEM allows consumers to export excess solar energy to the grid and offset against consumption
- National target: **70% renewable energy** in power generation mix by 2050 (NETR)

> **Citation**: SEDA Malaysia NEM Scheme

## Solaris Program

- Subsidies up to **RM4,000/kW** for first-time residential solar applicants
- Designed to make solar PV affordable for Malaysian households

> **Citation**: SEDA Solaris Program

## Solar PV Guidelines (Effective January 2025)

- **Guidelines for Solar Photovoltaic Installation for Self-Consumption** by Energy Commission (EC)
- Installations after 31 December 2024: subject to monthly standby charge of **RM14/kWp** for non-domestic installations exceeding **72 kWp**

> **Citation**: Energy Commission Guidelines for Solar PV Installation, effective 1 January 2025
