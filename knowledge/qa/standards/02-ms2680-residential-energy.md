# MS 2680:2017 -- Energy Efficiency (Residential)

## Overview

MS 2680:2017 is the companion standard to MS 1525, specifically for **residential buildings**. Primary performance indicators are OTTV and RTTV.

## Key Limits

| Parameter | Limit |
|-----------|-------|
| **OTTV** (Overall Thermal Transfer Value) | <= **50 W/m2** |
| **RTTV** (Roof Thermal Transfer Value) | <= **25 W/m2** |
| Roof U-value (light roof, <50 kg/m2) | **0.4 W/m2K** |
| Roof U-value (heavy roof, >50 kg/m2) | **0.6 W/m2K** |

> **Citation**: MS 2680:2017

## Malaysian Context

- Typical Malaysian residential OTTV varies from **35-65 W/m2**, with a mean of approximately **41.7 W/m2**
- Key influencing parameters:
  - **Window-to-wall ratio (WWR)**: major impact on OTTV
  - **Shading coefficient (SC)**: glass selection matters
  - **Wall U-value**: insulation of external walls
  - **Solar absorption**: colour and material of external surfaces

> **Citation**: MS 2680:2017
